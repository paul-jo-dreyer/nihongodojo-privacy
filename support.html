NihongoDojo — Support

App: NihongoDojo: Learn Japanese (dev.nihongodojo) · Provider: Paul Dreyer

NihongoDojo is a Japanese-language learning app — read authored stories, drill flashcards, practice typing, and chat with an AI tutor (Sensei). Need help or have a question? We're happy to hear from you.

Contact us: nihongodojoapp@gmail.com
We usually reply within 1–2 business days. Please include the email associated with your account and, for a purchase issue, the store receipt if you have it — it helps us resolve things faster.

Frequently asked questions

How do credits work?

The AI features (Sensei chat, word look-ups, and explanations) use credits. New accounts start with free welcome credits so you can try them right away. You can buy more anytime in Account → Buy credits, or subscribe for a monthly credit allowance. Reading stories, flashcards, and practice do not use credits.

I bought credits (or a book) but nothing showed up

Purchases are verified with the app store, which can take a moment. Try reopening the app first. If your credits or a book still haven't appeared, email us at nihongodojoapp@gmail.com with the store receipt and the email on your account, and we'll make it right.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed and managed by the app store, so cancellation happens there:

  • iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → NihongoDojo (or the in-app Manage subscription button, which opens the same place).
  • Android: Google Play → Menu → Subscriptions → NihongoDojo.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Sign in to the same account you used when purchasing and your credits, books, and subscription follow your account. If something is missing, contact us.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, open Account → Delete account and confirm — this permanently removes your account and associated data. If you've already uninstalled the app, email us from your account's email address and we'll delete it for you. (See the Privacy Policy for details.)

I'm having trouble signing in

NihongoDojo uses Google Sign-In on Android and Sign in with Apple on iPhone. Make sure you're signed into your device's Apple/Google account, then try again. Still stuck? Let us know what you see.

A book won't download

Book downloads need an internet connection. Check your connection and try again; you can also delete a book in My Books and re-download it from the Store.
