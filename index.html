NihongoDojo is a Japanese-language learning app. This policy explains what we collect, why, who we share it with, and your choices. We do not show ads, we do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers, and we do not sell your data.

What we collect

  • Account identity. When you sign in (Google on Android, Sign in with Apple on iOS) we receive a provider-issued account identifier and store it to operate your account. We do not store your email address or a password. Sign in with Apple's "Hide My Email" is fully supported.
  • Profile you provide. A display name, a unique @handle, and an avatar you choose. These are visible to other users you connect with.
  • Learning progress & social data. Your learning stats (such as level and words learned), your friend connections and requests, and flashcards you choose to send to or receive from friends. Basic stats (level, words learned, weekly activity) are visible to friends.
  • Purchases & credits. When you buy credits or a book, the app store (Google Play or Apple) processes the payment; we receive a purchase receipt/token and record your credit balance and which books you own so your purchases work across devices. We never receive or store your payment-card details.
  • Content you submit to AI features. Features like Sensei chat, word look-ups, and explanations send the text you enter to our processor (Anthropic) to generate a response. We do not retain the text of these messages on our servers — only anonymous usage counts needed to meter and bill credits.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, photos, advertising ID, or device identifiers for tracking.

How your information is used

  • To provide and operate the app (accounts, progress, credits, book downloads).
  • To power AI learning features (sending your input to Anthropic for processing).
  • To enable the social features you opt into (friends, shared stats, card gifting).
  • To process and restore your purchases and credit balance.
  • To protect the service (rate limiting and abuse prevention) and, if enabled, diagnose crashes.

Who we share it with (service providers)

  • Anthropic — processes the text you submit to AI features to generate responses. We send only what's needed to answer your request.
  • Google Play / Apple — process your payments and provide us a receipt to verify.
  • Sign-in providers (Google / Apple) — authenticate you and provide the account identifier.
  • Crash diagnostics (Sentry) — if enabled, receives crash/diagnostic reports to help us fix bugs.
  • Hosting — our backend; data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS.

We do not sell or rent personal data, and we do not share it for advertising.

Data retention & security

We keep account, profile, social, and entitlement data while your account exists. AI-feature message text is not retained by us. Data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS); session tokens are stored hashed at rest.

Your choices & account deletion

  • Edit your profile (name, @handle, avatar) in the app at any time.
  • Delete your account in-app: open Account → Delete account and confirm. This permanently removes your account and associated data from our servers and clears it from your device.
  • Delete by request: if you've uninstalled the app, email paul@reveredefense.co from the address associated with your account and we will delete your account and associated data.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data and to object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise them.

Children's privacy

NihongoDojo is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

International users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, which may have different data-protection laws. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; we will revise the effective date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app or via the store listing.
